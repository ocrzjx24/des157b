Futures Past.

The year is 2026. For generations, writers have imagined worlds beyond their own time. Some are hopeful, some terrifying, many uncannily accurate. Today, we find ourselves in the midst of the futures these writers predicted.

This archive orders speculative fiction not by when it was written, but by when it takes place, and seeks to observe and analyze each piece in regards to its accuracy with the real world.
